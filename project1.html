Development of Human Computer Interface for the Visually Impaired

Empathize Define System Analysis System Design Testing

Introduction

The world is largely visual. Television is a part of everyone’s lives. Computers and iPads are visual media. Statistically, communication is mainly visual. Research indicates that majority of communication happens visually and verbal expression remains limited. Today’s world must be made accessible for everyone, regardless of a person’s abilities or impairments. “Accessibility ensures people with disabilities can access the same information from a system as everyone else, and also gain the same benefits”.

The proposed project extends this idea of accessibility to physical interfaces, which will enable the visually challenged in various professional ways, to express their thoughts and ideas. Using Raspberry Pi, a simple yet efficient interface is developed which helps the visually challenged to convert an image of printed running text using Optical Character Recognition technology into speech. A Speech-to-Text feature is also embedded into this interface, which will convert a person’s speech into text and display the same onto a monitor.

Sidebar

How might we provide a fast and efficient mode of expression for people with visual impairment?

Interviews

I conducted 4 interviews with people of different age groups having different levels of visual impairments to understand different perspectives into the problem statement. During the interview, I found out how people needed a physical "model-like" interface especially to help them read understand different fonts and communicate using technology.

  1. What are the different ways in which you read documents?
  2. How do you feel using a mobile phone that can read out documents aloud to you?
  3. What problems do you face while assessing any document?

User Persona

Based on the interviews and analysis I conducted, I designed two personas of people with different demographics, age and personalities.

Empathy Map

After gathering inputs from interviews, personas and the insights, I got a list of what the user thinks, feels, does and says while shopping for an item. This empathy mapping helped me understand what the user actually wants and established the goals of this project

System Design

The detailed design of the proposed system is depicted using different diagrams like use case diagram, Activity diagram, Sequence diagram and Deployment diagram. It helps in understanding of how the system works and provides knowledge regarding the system.
