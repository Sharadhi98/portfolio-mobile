SnackIt: Snack Ordering App

SnackIt App is a snack ordering app for movie theatres in metropolitan areas. SnackIt brings all the snack and food stalls in a theatre under one umbrella and offers a wide spectrum of snacking options with attractive discounts and affordable prices. It tracks people who like to spend their movie breaks tending to other needs and delivers snacks at their seats.
